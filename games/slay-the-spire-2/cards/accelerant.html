Synergies & Strategies

This card accelerates The Silent's damage output immensely. By triggering Poison multiple times per turn, it bypasses the usual wait time, making it devastating against bosses or high-HP elites when you already have massive stacks of Poison applied.

Downside: Increased Poison Drain

Because Poison is reduced by 1 every time it triggers, Accelerant drains your poison stacks at an accelerated rate. Un-upgraded Accelerant removes 2 Poison per turn, and the upgraded version removes 3 per turn.
