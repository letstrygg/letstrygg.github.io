Slippery Bridge

Shared • Unknown Act

While crossing a rickety wooden bridge, there is a sudden torrent of rain. Massive gusts of wind buffet you, threatening to end your journey.

Choices / Outcomes

  • Hold On Lose 3+ HP. The card in the above option is randomized.
  • Overcome a random Card is removed from your Deck.
