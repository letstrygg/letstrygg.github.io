Jungle Maze Adventure

Event • Act 1 - Overgrowth

In a clearing, you run into a Ragtag Group of Adventurers looking down and gesturing at a gigantic maze.

They offer to join forces with you in an [b]Epic Quest[/b] to loot the riches of this maze. As an experienced adventurer yourself, it's clear this maze is filled with deadly traps and [orange]guardians[/orange].

It would be easier if you work together, but then you would have to split the loot.

Choices / Outcomes

  • Join Forces Gain 50 Gold.
  • Solo Quest Gain 150 Gold. Lose 18 HP.
