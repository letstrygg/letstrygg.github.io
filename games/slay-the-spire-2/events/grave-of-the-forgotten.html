Grave of the Forgotten

Event • Act 3 - Glory

There is a single grave with a [aqua]roiling blue flame[/aqua]...

A prideful warrior died in battle but its soul is restless. It wants to keep fighting, you feel it begging to journey with you.

But perhaps it's better for it to face the truth?

Choices / Outcomes

  • Accept the Forgotten Soul Obtain Forgotten Soul.
  • Confront with Truth Add Decay to your Deck. Enchant a card that Exhausts with Soul's Power.
  • Locked You have no cards with Exhaust that can be Enchanted.
