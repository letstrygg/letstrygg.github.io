Drowning Beacon

Event • Underdocks

Crossing a dreamscape of rocks and still waters, you emerge to face a sinking lighthouse emitting anti-light.

You can bottle up the [aqua]eerily-glowing water[/aqua] that has pooled here or climb up the dilapidated structure to retrieve the lens.

Choices / Outcomes
