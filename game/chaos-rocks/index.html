Chaos Rocks

Rock-throwing trick shots and Halloween chaos!

Watch Chaos Rocks gameplay on YouTube.

About the Game

Chaos Rocks is a quirky automation and physics-based puzzle game where you hurl rocks into buckets for points and trick shots. Use backboards, drones, and gravity wells to master each challenge and discover hidden secrets in a colorful, cel-shaded world.

The Halloween Demo offers a custom graveyard level with ghosts, pumpkins, and nostalgic Halloween vibes. Upgrade your tools, automate your score, and aim for ridiculous trick shots as you chase high scores and hidden skulls.

Gameplay Highlights

  • 🪨 Physics-based rock throwing with stylized, cel-shaded visuals
  • 🪵 Build elaborate trick shot setups with 9 unique backboards (wood & trampolines)
  • ⚙️ Automate your score with upgradeable drones
  • 🪣 Three unique bucket types: Point, Trick, Victory
  • 💀 Hidden skulls & bonus goals in each level
  • 🎯 Risk/reward gameplay and creative problem solving

Halloween Demo Details

The Chaos Rocks: Halloween Demo is a limited special version featuring:

  • 🎃 Custom graveyard level with ghosts and pumpkins
  • 💫 Trick Bucket – score big if you land it, no penalty if you miss
  • 🪵 9 backboards for creative bounces
  • 💀 Secret skull collectibles for bonus points
  • ⚠️ One level only (no achievements or controller support yet)
