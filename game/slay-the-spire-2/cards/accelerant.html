Keywords & Mechanics

Poison: Poisoned creatures lose HP at the start of their turn. Each turn, Poison is reduced by 1.

Synergies & Strategies

This card accelerates The Silent's damage output exponentially. Because it triggers Poison multiple times per turn rather than just applying more stacks, it bypasses the usual wait time, making it devastating against bosses or high-HP elites.
