Pacifist Challenge: Megabonk Guide

No Weapons Allowed — Defeat All Bosses

Video Guide

Overview

The Pacifist Challenge is one of Megabonk’s toughest trials — survive and defeat every boss without any weapons. Instead, rely on poison explosions, item synergies, and clever positioning to spread chaos.

Recommended Build

Character: Amog

Passive – Plague: Poisoned enemies explode on death, spreading poison to nearby foes.

Core Items

Cursed Doll: Curse an enemy, dealing 30% of their Max HP every second.


Moldy Cheese: +40% chance to poison enemies on hit.

Strategy

  • Prioritize finding Cursed Doll and Moldy Cheese early — they enable your poison chain.
  • Build defensively to survive while poison spreads.
  • Group smaller enemies near bosses to stack poison and trigger huge explosion chains.
