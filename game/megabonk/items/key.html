Key

+10% chance to open chests for free


The Key item in Megabonk gives a 10% chance for a chest to open for free. However, the chances stack *multiplicatively*, not additively — meaning 10 Keys does not guarantee 100%.

Video Explanation

How It Works

Each Key adds another independent 10% chance for the chest to open for free. The probability of a chest not opening for free with one Key is 90% (0.9). With multiple Keys, you multiply that chance together: 

Formula: Chance to open = 1 − (0.9)n

Where n is the number of Keys you have.
