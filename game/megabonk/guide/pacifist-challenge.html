Pacifist Challenge

No Weapons Allowed — Defeat All Bosses

Overview

The Pacifist Challenge is one of Megabonk’s toughest runs — you must survive and kill every boss without equipping any weapons. Instead, you'll rely on poison explosions, item synergies, and clever positioning to spread chaos.

Recommended Build

Character: Amog

Core Items

Strategy

  • Prioritize finding Cursed Doll and Moldy Cheese early — they enable your poison chain.
  • Build defensive layers to survive while poison spreads.
  • Group smaller enemies around bosses to stack poison faster and trigger huge explosion chains.
