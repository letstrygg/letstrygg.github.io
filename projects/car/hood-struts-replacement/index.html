How to Replace Hood Struts

Acura TL (2004–2006) • Applies to most vehicles

Quick DIY Hood Strut Replacement ~3 min
If your hood won’t stay up, this fast repair shows how to replace worn hood struts with minimal tools.
Tools & Parts
  • Replacement hood struts (pair)
  • Small flathead screwdriver
  • Optional: vice grip pliers
  • Optional: work gloves
Hood Struts • Screwdriver • Vice Grips
Steps
  1. Open hood and support it by hand or with vice grips.
  2. Pry the metal retaining clip outward with a flathead screwdriver.
  3. Pop the old strut off the ball stud.
  4. Press the new strut onto the ball stud until it snaps.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
Safety Notes
The hood is heavy. Always keep one hand on it while removing a strut to prevent injury.
