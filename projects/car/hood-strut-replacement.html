Replace Hood Struts

Acura TL (2004–2006) • Applies to most cars


Video Guide

Overview

If your hood no longer stays open, worn hood struts are the cause. This is a fast, beginner‑friendly repair that takes just a few minutes per side.

Tools & Parts

  • Replacement hood struts (pair) – Amazon
  • Small flathead screwdriver – Amazon
  • Optional: small vice grips – Amazon
  • Optional: work gloves – Amazon

Step‑by‑Step

  1. Secure the hood. Open it fully and support it with one hand or a prop.
  2. Find the retaining clips. Each strut end is held on by a small metal clip.
  3. Pry the clip outward. Use a small flathead screwdriver—do not remove it completely.
  4. Pop the strut off. Pull it straight off the ball stud while supporting the hood.
  5. Install the new strut. Press it onto the ball stud until it snaps into place.
  6. Repeat on the other side.

Final Check

Slowly close and reopen the hood to confirm both struts are seated and the hood stays open on its own.
