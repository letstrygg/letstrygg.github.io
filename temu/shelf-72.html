Verdict

These shelves — Temu or Home Depot — are all in the same class: light-duty, MDF-shelf units that work well for home storage, tools, gaming gear, or general organization. They are absolutely not suited for heavy-duty industrial loads. But for normal home usage, the Temu one does the job just as well for a tiny fraction of the price.

If someone really wants stronger shelves, it's better to buy a heavier-duty system entirely rather than trying to force these to do a job they were never designed for.

For what they are, the Temu shelves are a solid value.
