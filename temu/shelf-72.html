72" Steel Shelving Unit – Temu Review

I paid $27.98 for two. Home Depot charges $104.99 each.

On November 7th, 2025 I purchased a pair of 72" steel shelving units from Temu for $27.98 total, free shipping. As of November 14th, 2025, the equivalent Husky/HDX unit at Home Depot is $104.99 each before tax.

Despite a few small differences—mainly slightly thinner metal and plastic connectors—the shelves are fully functional and surprisingly close in real-world usage to my existing big-box shelves. I already owned eight similar units from Home Depot, and these integrate well with them.

After assembling the first two, I immediately bought four more sets.

At ~$14 per shelf delivered, they're one of the best value purchases I’ve made in years.
